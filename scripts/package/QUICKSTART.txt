LinBoard — Quick Start
======================

1. Extract this folder
2. Run: ./install.sh
3. Start: linboard
4. Press Super+V (Win key + V) to open clipboard history

Commands:
  linboard              Start (system tray)
  linboard toggle       Show/hide history
  linboard install-shortcut   Re-register Super+V

Works on: GNOME, KDE Plasma, XFCE, Cinnamon, X11 and Wayland.

Support: https://github.com/Foisalislambd/LinBoard
